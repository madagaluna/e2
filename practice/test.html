Mary's Favorite Things

Tunes, Timber, and (Daydreaming about) Travel

Tunes

I have no musical talent but music plays a pretty significant role in my life. Playing trombone started off as a hobby and grew into a northstar for me when I experienced the power music has to builds community.

  • I play trombone with School of HONK. When there are 40 trombonists around you, it doesn't matter if you hit a wrong note. Being part of the band brings me tremendous joy. I'd love to be able to play better, of course, but there are a million things I'd love to do better. So, I forget about the wrong notes and just enjoy myself.
  • I started Belmont Porchfest to encourage everyone to play music
  • I made a documentary about Porchfests to inspire others. Here is a link to the tickets of the premiere!

Timber

Timber is a COVID pup. He is a joy. I take him on an adventure everyday.

  • Somedays I take Timber to Fresh Pond. He runs while I ride a scooter - like the kids ride. We both have big goofy smiles the whole time.
  • Somedays I walk him up to the Conservation Land in Belmont. .
  • Sometimes I take him to Cat Rock, where he can go swimming. Scratch that, he goes fishing. No kidding. He's more obsessed with the sunfish than tennis balls.

Travel
